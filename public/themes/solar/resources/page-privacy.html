Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1 — The Short Version

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • It does not send information to me or to any third party.
  • It does not include analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.
  • It does not “phone home” for any reason.

What you do in the app stays on your device (and, if you choose, your personal iCloud account).

2 — Data Collection

I do not collect any data from you.

  • No personal information
  • No location data sent to me
  • No usage data
  • No analytics
  • No crash reports

If the app records information (such as location data), that data is stored only on your device or in your personal iCloud storage, under your control.

3 — Third Parties

This app does not share data with anyone.

  • No third-party analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No external servers
  • No data brokers

There is no mechanism in the app to transmit your data to any external system.

4 — Network Activity

This app does not send data over the internet.

If your device is offline, the app works the same as when it is online.

Any network activity that may occur is handled by Apple’s systems (for example, iCloud syncing if you choose to use it), not by me.

5 — Your Data

You own your data.

Files created by the app are stored in your iCloud Drive or on your device

  • You can access, move, or delete them at any time
  • You do not need permission from me to use your own data
  • The app uses standard file formats so your data is not locked in.

6 — Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected, this app does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

7 — Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the changes will be simple and clearly stated.

However, the core principle will not change: your data stays with you.
